Frequently asked questions

How do I get started?
Simply click "Launch Live App" above to access the platform. No registration required - start analyzing immediately.
What data sources are supported?
Upload your own CSV files or use our synthetic data generator. We support user events, signup data, and custom metrics.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all data processing happens in your browser. We don't store or transmit your data to external servers.
Can I export my analysis?
Absolutely! All charts, tables, and reports can be exported as CSV files for further analysis or sharing.
